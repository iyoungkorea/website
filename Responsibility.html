Our Commitment to Responsibility

At YoungKorea, we recognize that sustainable growth is inseparable from ethical governance, environmental stewardship, and social accountability. Our responsibility strategy aligns with international standards to ensure long-term value creation for all stakeholders—including our customers, employees, communities, and future generations.


ESG Principles

Environmental

  • Adoption of energy-efficient equipment and processes
  • Waste and emissions reduction through optimized supply chain and logistics
  • Promoting recycling and sustainable packaging
  • Compliance with RoHS and REACH directives
  • Regular environmental impact assessments

Social

  • Fair labor practices and safe working conditions across the supply chain
  • Equal opportunity hiring and diversity promotion
  • Mandatory compliance training for all employees
  • Community engagement through education and local development programs
  • Protection of human rights in all operations

Governance

  • Transparent reporting and independent audits
  • Board diversity and clear separation of executive roles
  • Zero-tolerance policy for corruption, bribery, or unethical behavior
  • Active compliance with anti-trust and trade regulations
  • Clear channels for whistleblower protection and investigation

Code of Ethics

Our Code of Ethics governs internal and external conduct and ensures alignment with international legal frameworks and market expectations.

  • Zero tolerance for bribery or unethical conduct
  • Full compliance with GDPR and customer data protection laws
  • Prohibition of insider trading and conflicts of interest
  • Fair competition and anti-monopoly compliance
  • Confidentiality and integrity in all communications
  • Immediate investigation of whistleblower reports without retaliation

Certifications and Memberships

  • Compliance with ISO and RoHS standards
  • Ongoing participation in industry-wide sustainability initiatives
  • Voluntary reporting aligned with global ESG frameworks

Future Goals

  • Establish full ESG performance tracking and public disclosure by 2026
  • Expand green logistics and carbon offset programs
  • Transition 50% of packaging to sustainable materials by 2025
  • Implement supplier ESG audits annually
