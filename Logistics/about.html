Our Roots in Maehwa industrial Complex

Though the Maehwa Industrial Complex itself was officially established in 2015, the expertise it houses spans decades. Many of the companies now operating within the complex have been pillars of the regional economy for 20 to 40 years, formerly scattered across Siheung-si and surrounding districts. The creation of the Maehwa Complex was part of a broader regional initiative to modernize infrastructure, optimize logistics, and foster industrial cooperation. It provided these longstanding businesses a centralized, upgraded environment in which to collaborate and grow.


____________________________________________________


A Cohesive Industrial Community

These companies—specializing in mechanical components, renewable energy, urban infrastructure, and high-precision manufacturing—relocated to Maehwa not out of necessity but out of shared ambition. They brought with them deep-rooted knowledge, patented technologies, and long-standing reputations. Now working side by side, they benefit from shared resources, logistical synergy, and community-led innovation.


____________________________________________________


Bringing Generations and Markets

As the next generation stepping into the legacy of our predecessors, we recognize the challenges faced by our community in navigating the global market. Language barriers and limited exposure have often hindered these businesses from showcasing their capabilities internationally. YoungKorea Logistics aims to overcome these challenges by providing a platform that highlights their products, stories, and values, connecting them with global partners and opportunities—translating their legacy into global relevance. Language, marketing, and cross-border negotiation should not be barriers to quality.

Our Vision

YoungKorea Logistics doesn’t just support business—it supports generational continuity, collective advancement, and technical excellence. Our goal is to create a new industrial narrative where seasoned manufacturers meet modern distribution and digital visibility. The Maehwa Industrial Complex is more than a location—it’s a declaration of continued relevance and shared future.
