Our Roots in Maehwa industrial Complex

Though the Maehwa Industrial Complex itself was officially established in 2015, the expertise it houses spans decades. Many of the companies now operating within the complex have been pillars of the regional economy for 20 to 40 years, formerly scattered across Siheung-si and surrounding districts. The creation of the Maehwa Complex was part of a broader regional initiative to modernize infrastructure, optimize logistics, and foster industrial cooperation. It provided these longstanding businesses a centralized, upgraded environment in which to collaborate and grow.
