Our Commitment to Responsibility

At YoungKorea, we believe that long-term business success is built on integrity, transparency, and respect for our stakeholders and the planet. Our commitment to responsible management extends across all levels of our operations—from our environmental practices to our corporate governance and social responsibility.

ESG Principles

Environmental

  • Implementing energy-efficient manufacturing processes
  • Minimizing waste and emissions through smart logistics
  • Promoting recycling and sustainable packaging

Social

  • Ensuring fair labor practices and employee safety
  • Promoting diversity and inclusion within our workforce
  • Engaging with local communities and supporting public welfare

Governance

  • Upholding transparency and accountability in all operations
  • Maintaining an independent and ethical board structure
  • Adhering strictly to anti-corruption and compliance regulations

Code of Ethics

Our Code of Ethics reflects our dedication to fairness, responsibility, and corporate integrity. It guides our decisions and behaviors as we pursue business goals.

  • Zero tolerance for bribery or unethical conduct
  • Commitment to customer privacy and data protection
  • Transparency in communication and reporting
  • Responsiveness to whistleblower concerns
  • Honest competition and anti-trust compliance
